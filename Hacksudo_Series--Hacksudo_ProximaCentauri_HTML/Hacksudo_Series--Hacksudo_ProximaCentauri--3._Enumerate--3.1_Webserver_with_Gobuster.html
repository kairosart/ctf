3.1 Webserver with Gobuster



Discover the directory and files on the webserver.
1. On your Kali Machine run the following command.

$gobuster dir -u http://192.168.12.34 -x txt,php,html --wordlist /usr/share/wordlists/dirbuster/directory-list-2.3-medium.txt -z


Output:


There's a directory called “/planet”.

2. Navigate to http://192.168.12.34/planet.


3. Click on the “travel/” directory.


Apparently there's not anything interesting.

4. Right-click and choose the “View page source” option.


You'll see a message to get the coordinates on a website and the way of open and close The Proxima Blackhole porta.

5. Look up on google “proxima centauri coordinates”.
